# Realistic Photo Tip (gokayfem)

Source: @gokayfem on X/Twitter (Dec 24, 2025)

## Usage

Append this to the end of any prompt to make images more realistic with
Gemini image models (Nano Banana Pro / gemini-3-pro-image-preview).

## Prompt Suffix

> The image is a hyper-realistic documentary photograph captured with a
> Sony A7R V and a 35mm prime lens, stopped down to f/11 for a deep
> depth of field where every detail from foreground to background remains
> tack-sharp.

## Key Specs

- **Camera:** Sony A7R V
- **Lens:** 35mm prime lens
- **Aperture:** f/11
- **Style:** Hyper-realistic documentary photograph
- **Depth of field:** Deep, everything tack-sharp

## Notes

- Works best for realistic/documentary style images
- The camera/lens specificity helps the model ground the image in
  photographic realism
- Original tip was truncated in the tweet; the core technique is
  specifying a real camera model + lens + aperture settings
